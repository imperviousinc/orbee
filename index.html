What makes it different

It's yours, not theirs.

Discord and Telegram are great, until they're not. One policy change, and your community is gone. Orbee's built so that can't happen.

A name for life

Pick a handle like myname@orbee and it's yours forever. Nobody can take it, rename it, or lock you out - not orbee, not anyone.

Host it yourself

A group lives on a server. Run your own for your friends, or join groups on public ones. Disagree with the host? Start your own, bring your name.

Open world

Built on Nostr and Spaces - a set of standards for an open internet. Talk to anyone on any compatible app. No walled gardens.
